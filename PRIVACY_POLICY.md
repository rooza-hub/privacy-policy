# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** January 2025

## Introduction

Rooza ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use the Rooza browser extension.

By using Rooza, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

## Information We Collect

### Data Stored Locally

Rooza stores the following information locally in your browser using the browser's extension storage API:

- **Widget Layouts**: Your custom widget positions and arrangements for mobile, tablet, and desktop devices
- **Theme Preferences**: Your selected theme (light, dark, blue, or green)
- **Language Settings**: Your preferred language (English or Persian/Farsi)
- **Bookmarks**: Bookmarks you create through the extension (title, URL, creation date)
- **Weather Preferences**: Your selected city and temperature unit preference (Celsius or Fahrenheit)
- **Onboarding Status**: Whether you have completed the initial setup process
- **Settings**: Other customization preferences you configure

**All this data is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to our servers or third parties.**

### Data Retrieved from Browser APIs

Rooza uses the following browser APIs to provide functionality:

- **Bookmarks API**: To display and manage your browser bookmarks
- **Search API**: To enable web search functionality from the search bar

### Data Retrieved from Third-Party Services

Rooza may fetch data from the following third-party services:

- **OpenWeatherMap API**: To retrieve weather information for your selected city. This includes:
  - Current temperature and weather conditions
  - Weather forecasts
  - Location data (city name, country)
  
  **Note**: Weather API requests include your selected city's coordinates (latitude/longitude) but do not include any personally identifiable information.

- **Google Search Suggestions**: The extension may fetch search suggestions from Google's autocomplete service when you type in the search bar. This is done through Google's public API and is subject to Google's privacy policy.

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely to:

1. **Provide Core Functionality**: Display your customized dashboard, bookmarks, weather, calendar, and events
2. **Maintain Preferences**: Remember your settings and layout preferences across browser sessions
3. **Enable Features**: Support search functionality, bookmark management, and weather display
4. **Improve User Experience**: Ensure the extension works as expected with your preferences

## Data Storage and Security

### Local Storage

All user preferences and data are stored locally in your browser using the Chrome Extension Storage API. This data:
- Remains on your device
- Is not transmitted to external servers
- Is encrypted by your browser's storage system
- Can be deleted by uninstalling the extension or clearing extension data

### Third-Party Data

When fetching weather data or search suggestions:
- API requests are made directly from your browser to third-party services
- We do not intercept, store, or log these requests
- Third-party services may have their own privacy policies and data collection practices

## Third-Party Services

Rooza integrates with the following third-party services:

### OpenWeatherMap

- **Purpose**: Weather data retrieval
- **Data Shared**: City coordinates (latitude/longitude), language preference
- **Privacy Policy**: [https://openweathermap.org/privacy-policy](https://openweathermap.org/privacy-policy)

### Google Services

- **Purpose**: Search suggestions and web search
- **Data Shared**: Search queries (when you perform a search)
- **Privacy Policy**: [https://policies.google.com/privacy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)

### Nager Date API (Reserved for Future Use)

- **Purpose**: Public holidays and events data (currently not in use)
- **Privacy Policy**: [https://date.nager.at/Privacy](https://date.nager.at/Privacy)

We recommend reviewing the privacy policies of these third-party services to understand how they handle your data.

## Data Sharing

**We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties.** 

The only data transmission occurs when:
- You perform a web search (data goes to your selected search engine)
- The extension fetches weather data (city coordinates sent to OpenWeatherMap)
- The extension fetches search suggestions (queries sent to Google's autocomplete service)

4. **Opt-Out**: Stop using the extension at any time by uninstalling it
