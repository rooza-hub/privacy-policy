# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: June 2025**

This is the privacy policy for **Rooza**, the new‑tab browser extension. The live version is available at [rooza.space/en/privacy](https://rooza.space/en/privacy).

## What data is processed

Rooza only processes the data needed for its widgets to work — such as your settings (widget layout, language and weather city). This data is kept locally on your device.

## Third-party services

Some widgets (such as weather or currency & gold rates) send requests to external services to show live data. Those services have their own privacy policies.

## Selling or sharing

We do not sell your personal data, nor share it with third parties for advertising purposes.

## Your control

You can clear Rooza's stored data or uninstall the extension at any time from your browser settings.

## Contact

For any privacy questions, reach us via the [Contact page](https://rooza.space/en/contact).
